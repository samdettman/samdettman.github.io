About the character

Hank Schrader is Walter's brother-in-law and a DEA agent who is tasked with investigating the meth trade in Albuquerque. He's a tough, smart cop who is determined to bring down Heisenberg, but as he gets closer to the truth, he finds himself in grave danger.
